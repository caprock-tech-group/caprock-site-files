Operating Doctrine

/ Defined Deployment Standards /

Deployment & Commissioning
Every Solar Sentry deployment includes site assessment, placement optimization, mast extension, camera calibration, AI detection tuning, connectivity verification, and full system commissioning. This ensures the system is evidence-ready from day one.

Authorized Movement Policy
Solar Sentry units may ONLY be relocated or re-commissioned by an Authorized Caprock Technician. Because precision calibration is critical for AI tracking and solar harvest, unauthorized movement immediately suspends monitoring services and voids the warranty.

Monitoring Plans
Every unit operates under a managed service plan ranging from connectivity-only (Pipeline) to full incident handling and police handoff (Detective). Active monitoring is required for real-time target deterrence.

Warranty Coverage Rules
The 3-Year Commercial Warranty covers all mechanical and electronic components provided the unit remains on a Sentry or Detective plan and all moves are authorized. Hardware failures are replaced with priority dispatch to ensure zero site downtime.

Warranty Integrity Warning

The 3-Year Commercial Warranty is active only on units with Sentry or Detective plans. This warranty is voided by unauthorized relocation; all moves must be performed by an Authorized Caprock Technician.
