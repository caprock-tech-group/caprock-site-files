Our Managed Surveillance Philosophy

It's crucial to understand our role. Caprock Technology Group provides **Managed Surveillance Systems**, which means we proactively monitor the health and reliability of your security equipment to ensure it is operational. **We do not provide active human monitoring of your video feeds.** All camera alerts, such as motion detection, are sent directly to you, the client, via the mobile app. Our focus is on maintaining the technology so you can have confidence in your security.
