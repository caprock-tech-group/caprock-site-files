Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 12, 2026

At Caprock Technology Group, we are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our clients. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal and business information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our IT services, including:

  • Contact Information: Names, email addresses, phone numbers, and physical addresses.
  • Business Data: Company details, domain names, and user counts provided during onboarding.
  • Technical Data: Information gathered by our monitoring tools (Atera, Bitdefender) necessary for system health checks, security, and asset management.
  • Payment Information: Billing details collected securely through our payment processors. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely for the purpose of delivering and improving our services:

  • Service Delivery: To provision accounts, deploy software, and manage your IT infrastructure.
  • Support: To respond to your helpdesk tickets and inquiries.
  • Communication: To send critical alerts, invoices, and service updates.
  • Compliance: To meet legal obligations, such as A2P 10DLC registration for messaging services.

3. Data Protection & Sharing

Security: We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your data.

No Sale of Data: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Vendors: We may share necessary data with trusted third-party providers (e.g., Atera, Bitdefender, Acronis, mobile carriers) solely to facilitate the services you have purchased. These vendors are contractually obligated to protect your data.

4. Messaging & SMS Policy

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive text messages from us regarding your inquiries, service updates, or account notifications. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying "STOP."

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

5. Your Rights & Contact

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data held by us, subject to legal and contractual retention requirements.
