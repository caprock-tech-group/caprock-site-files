About Our Founder

Nick Stevens

My name is Nick Stevens, and I founded Caprock Technology Group with a single, clear mission: to provide the kind of strategic, proactive IT partnership that I saw was missing in the Texas Panhandle.

With over 10 years of experience in the technology field, my most recent role as an Information Technology Specialist at a local credit union gave me a deep understanding of environments where security and reliability are non-negotiable. In that role, I saw firsthand that the tools exist to prevent most downtime before it ever happens. The problem was, too many small and medium-sized businesses were stuck in a reactive "break-fix" cycle, dealing with constant tech headaches and unpredictable costs.

I knew there was a better way. Caprock Technology Group was built on the philosophy that technology should be a competitive advantage, not a liability. We use a modern, AI-driven platform to monitor your systems 24/7 and prevent issues before they can impact your operations. Our goal is to give you the peace of mind that comes from knowing your technology is stable, secure, and aligned with your business goals.

When I'm not helping our partners with their technology, I enjoy going to church and experimenting with new tech projects.

I started this business to build genuine, long-term relationships and help local businesses thrive. If you're tired of dealing with tech surprises and are ready for a true IT partner, I'd be happy to schedule a brief discovery call to learn more about your business.

Your strategic IT partner in the Texas Panhandle.
