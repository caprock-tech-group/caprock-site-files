A Message from Our Founder

My name is Nick Stevens, and I founded Caprock Technology Group to bring a new level of proactive, strategic IT partnership to the businesses that form the backbone of our community here in the Texas Panhandle.

I've spent over a decade in the technology field, with my most recent role helping to manage and secure the critical systems of a local credit union. That experience in a high-stakes, security-focused environment showed me firsthand that the tools and strategies exist to prevent most IT problems before they can disrupt a business. Yet, too many local companies were still stuck in a reactive "break-fix" cycle, waiting for things to fail before getting help.

Caprock Technology Group was built on the philosophy that your technology should be a competitive advantage, not a liability. My mission is to provide the proactive management and strategic guidance you need to not just fix problems, but to make your technology a powerful engine for growth and efficiency.

When I'm not focused on technology, I enjoy spending time with my family, serving at my church, and experimenting with new tech projects. I believe in building long-term relationships based on trust and tangible results, and I look forward to the opportunity to learn about your business.
