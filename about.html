About Caprock Technology Group

A local partner dedicated to turning your technology into a competitive advantage.

The Caprock Standard

My name is Nick Stevens, and I founded Caprock Technology Group to move Texas Panhandle businesses out of the reactive "break-fix" cycle and into an era of Redundant Reliability.

With over a decade in technology and a background securing critical infrastructure for local financial institutions, I've seen firsthand that security is not an afterthought—it is the foundation of every successful operation. I built Caprock to bring that same high-stakes, security-focused discipline to your jobsite and your office.

As an Authorized Dealer for the Montavue Solar Sentry, I specialize in deploying autonomous, American-made defense grids designed specifically for the rugged demands of the construction and heavy equipment industries. We don't just sell hardware; we provide a technical partnership that eliminates the 'rental loop' and turns your security into a measurable competitive advantage.

When I'm not engineering new defense solutions, I stay grounded through my family, serving at my church, and maintaining the physical discipline required to lead at a high level. At Caprock, we don't just fix problems—we prevent them.
